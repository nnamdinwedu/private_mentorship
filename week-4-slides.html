WEEK 4:

Confirmation-Based Entry System

Precision Entry, Smart Execution

Lesson Objective

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Confidently use the 3-Layered Entry System.
  • Understand candlestick logic and momentum to confirm entries.
  • Spot and use inducement traps to your advantage.
  • Use a clear checklist to execute trades with confidence.

1. The 3-Layered Entry System

Think of it like a funnel: from wide to razor-sharp.

Layer 1: Buffer Entry

A small position at the higher timeframe POI to ensure you don't miss the move entirely.

Layer 2: Refined Entry

A tighter entry zone found within the POI using a lower timeframe FVG or CSD.

Layer 3: Confirmation Entry

The most precise entry, taken after a BOS on the entry timeframe, confirming the direction.

2. Candlestick Logic & Momentum

Candles tell a story of institutional intent. We learn to read it.

Momentum Candles

Long-bodied candles that break structure with conviction. They confirm a strong move, especially when they close beyond a zone, not just wick through it.

Opposite Candles

Candles that go against the prevailing trend within a zone. These are often signals for inducement traps or the final test before a confirmation entry.

3. Inducement & Trap Setups

Inducement is a key piece of the puzzle. It's how Smart Money clears out the early birds.

What is Inducement?

A move designed to tempt retail traders into an entry too early, only to reverse and hit their stop loss before the real move happens.

How to Use It:

Your entry comes after the inducement is cleared. You wait for the trap to complete, then strike with confidence.

Inducement is like a fishing lure. Smart Money dangles it to catch the impatient traders. Your job is to be the fisherman, not the fish.

4. Your System Checklist

Before you take a trade, ensure you can tick these boxes:

  • ✓ POI is a strong, unmitigated zone.
  • ✓ Market has swept liquidity before hitting the zone.
  • ✓ Candlestick shows strong momentum.
  • ✓ Entry timeframe has a BOS with an FVG/CSD.
  • ✓ Inducement has been cleared.
  • ✓ Reward is at least 1:2 Minimum.

"You don’t need to catch every move — just catch the clean ones. The market pays patience."
