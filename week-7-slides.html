WEEK 7:

(Get Funded) Prop Firm Preparation

Your Final Step to Becoming a Professional Trader

Lesson Objective

By the end of this week, you will be able to:

  • Understand the psychology of prop firm challenges.
  • Review and adapt your trading plan for prop firm rules.
  • Confidently pass your first funded evaluation.
  • Receive your final evaluation and be ready to trade live.

1. Prop Firm Psychology

The rules of a prop firm are not just about trading; they're about managing yourself under pressure.

Key Concepts:

  • Daily Drawdown: Why this rule is a test of your discipline, not your strategy.
  • Max Drawdown: The ultimate test of capital protection.
  • Managing Pressure: How to trade a funded account the same way you trade a demo account.

2. Adapting Your Trading Plan

Your system is ready. Now we make it fit the prop firm rules.

Your Plan Must Include:

  • Strict rules for managing daily and max drawdown.
  • A clear profit target and what to do after it's hit.
  • A plan for when to stop trading for the day/week to avoid violating rules.

Your trading plan is your constitution. In a prop firm challenge, you are the law, and you must follow it flawlessly.

3. Live Trade Review & Final Evaluation

This is where we put everything to the test.

Final Evaluation:

  • We'll do live, real-time feedback sessions.
  • You will walk through a trade, and we will analyze it together.
  • Your final evaluation is a discussion on your journal, your plan, and your psychological readiness.

This is your graduation. You're not just passing a course; you're proving to yourself and your mentors that you are ready for the next step.

Final Note to Mentees

Congratulations!

"You came here to learn a system. You're leaving as a confident, disciplined trader."
