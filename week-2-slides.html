2. Understanding Market Structure

The market isn't random. It moves in a predictable structure.

Core Concepts:

  • Highs & Lows: The basic building blocks of trend.
  • Higher Highs (HH) & Higher Lows (HL): An uptrend is forming.
  • Lower Highs (LH) & Lower Lows (LL): A downtrend is forming.

BOS & CHoCH

These are the signals that tell you the trend is continuing or changing.

Break of Structure (BOS)

A new high/low that continues the current trend.

Think of it as a train staying on its track. It breaks through old barriers to keep going in the same direction.

Change of Character (CHoCH)

A new high/low that breaks the existing trend, signaling a potential reversal.

This is when the train switches tracks. It's a signal to pay attention, as the direction might be changing.

3. Liquidity & Inducement

Forget indicators. Price moves to hunt liquidity.

Liquidity

Simply put, it's where other traders have their stop losses placed. Smart Money needs this liquidity to fill their orders.

Inducement

A deliberate move by the market to trick retail traders into a bad entry, taking their liquidity before the real move happens.

"Price doesn’t just move—it hunts. You’re either the hunted or the hunter."

4. Why Most Traders Fail

The biggest battle is not with the market, but with yourself.

Key Causes of Failure:

  • Chasing price without a structured plan.
  • Emotional attachment to outcomes (wins and losses).
  • Ignorance of how the market actually works (market logic).
